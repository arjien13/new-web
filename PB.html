Siquijor Trip

"I'm thrilled to share my second adventure to the enchanting island of Siquijor, a trip that was all about carefree exploration and reconnecting with friends. At the invitation of some dear friends who call Siquijor home, I embarked on a journey to discover the island's hidden gems. We opted for a relaxed approach, renting a cozy room at a local resort and spending our days meandering around the island, soaking up its natural beauty and warm atmosphere. Every moment was a treasure, and I'm already counting down the days until my next visit. Siquijor, I'll be back soon!"
